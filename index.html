Whack-a-mole! Track your score

0

  • The original version of this game was provided by Fullstack Academy.
    • It tracked your score through the same page view.
    • This meant that, if you refreshed the page, you'd lose your score.
  • Currently, your score is tracked through sessionStorage.
    • Even if you refresh the page, your score stays the same.
    • However, if you open this page on a new tab, the score gets reset back to zero.
  • With your permission, we can track your score across tabs using cookies!
