Track your score: 0

  • The original version of this game was provided by Fullstack Academy.
    • It tracked your score on the same page view.
    • If you refreshed the page, you'd lose your score.
  • Currently, your score is tracked through sessionStorage.
    • With sessionStorage, we can keep tracking your score even if you refresh the page.
    • However, if you open this page on a new tab, the score gets reset back to zero.
  • To fix this, we can track your score across tabs using cookies!
    • This means that you can open a new tab and your score will be saved.
    • Note: This will only work through regular browser sessions and not incognito mode.
